Qazaqstan, Qaton-Qaragai

One of the most mysterious places on the planet is Altai. The extreme eastern point of the country - Katon-Karagai district occupies the northeastern part of the territory of the East Kazakhstan region and is located at the junction of the borders of the two states. In the north and northeast, the district borders with the Ust-Koksinsky and Kosh-Agachsky districts of the Altai Republic of the Russian Federation, in the southeast with the Altai District of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China. The distance from the extreme eastern point of the district to the western borders of Mongolia is about 40 km.

RELIEF, CLIMATE

The terrain of the area is predominantly mountainous. On all sides, Katon-Karagay is surrounded by mountain ranges:

  • Narymsky
  • Sarymsakty
  • Tarbagatai
  • Southern Altai
  • Listvyaga
  • Katunsky
